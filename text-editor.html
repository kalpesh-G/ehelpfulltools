About the Online Text Editor

The Enhanced Text Formatter is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help users create, edit, and format text efficiently. Whether you're a student, writer, or professional, this tool provides essential formatting options for crafting well-structured content.

How to Use This Tool:

  • 🔹 Enter or upload your text file.
  • 🔹 Apply bold, italic, or underline styling to selected text.
  • 🔹 Choose from different font styles, colors, and sizes.
  • 🔹 Align text left, center, or right.
  • 🔹 Add bullet points or numbered lists.
  • 🔹 Use Find & Replace to quickly edit your content.
  • 🔹 Copy or download the formatted text for future use.

Why eHelpfulTools Exists

We started eHelpfulTools in 2020 with a simple mission: to create free, accessible tools that solve everyday problems without requiring signups or payments.
