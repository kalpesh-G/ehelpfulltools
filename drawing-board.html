About the Advanced Drawing Board

The Advanced Drawing Board is a feature-rich and user-friendly tool that lets you create digital artwork effortlessly. Whether you're an artist, designer, student, or just someone who loves doodling, this tool provides a seamless way to sketch, draw, and design with ease.

How to Use This Tool:

  • 🔹 Choose a Brush: Select from multiple brush types like pen, pencil, and marker.
  • 🔹 Pick Colors: Use the color picker to choose any shade you like.
  • 🔹 Adjust Brush Size & Opacity: Customize your strokes for detailed or bold designs.
  • 🔹 Use Layers & Shapes: Draw freehand or add predefined shapes (lines, rectangles, circles, etc.).
  • 🔹 Eraser & Undo/Redo: Easily fix mistakes or refine your artwork.
  • 🔹 Save Your Work: Download your drawing as an image file (PNG or JPG).
