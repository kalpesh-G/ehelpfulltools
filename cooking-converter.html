🔄 Quick Ingredient Substitutes

  • 1 कप मक्खन = ½ कप तेल + ½ कप सेब की चटनी
  • 1 कप चीनी = ¾ कप शहद या मेपल सिरप
  • 1 अंडा = ¼ कप मैश किया हुआ केला या सेब की चटनी
  • 1 कप छाछ = 1 कप दूध + 1 बड़ा चम्मच सिरका
  • 1 बड़ा चम्मच ताज़ा जड़ी-बूटी = 1 छोटा चम्मच सूखी जड़ी-बूटी
  • 1 cup of butter = ½ cup oil + ½ cup applesauce
  • 1 cup of sugar = ¾ cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 egg = ¼ cup mashed banana or applesauce
  • 1 cup buttermilk = 1 cup milk + 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp fresh herbs = 1 tsp dried herbs

About the Advanced Cooking Measurement Converter 🍽️

The Advanced Cooking Measurement Converter is your ultimate kitchen assistant, designed to help you convert ingredient measurements, adjust temperatures, and find quick ingredient substitutes effortlessly.

How to Use This Tool:

  • 🔹 Enter the quantity of an ingredient you want to convert.
  • 🔹 Select the unit you want to convert from and to (e.g., Cups → Grams).
  • 🔹 Click Convert to see the result instantly.
  • 🔹 Use the Temperature Converter for baking and cooking adjustments.
  • 🔹 Check the Substitutes List if you are missing an ingredient.
