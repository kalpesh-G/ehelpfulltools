Our Story

Why eHelpfulTools Exists

We started eHelpfulTools in 2020 with a simple mission: to create free, accessible tools that solve everyday problems without requiring signups or payments.

Frustrated by websites that demanded personal information just to use basic tools, we built a collection of utilities that respect your privacy and work instantly.

Today, eHelpfulTools serves thousands of users worldwide - students, teachers, freelancers, and professionals who value simplicity and efficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to use the tools? ▼

No! All our tools are completely free to use without any account creation. Just visit the tool you need and start using it immediately.

Is there a limit to how much I can use the tools? ▼

No limits at all. Use our tools as much as you need, whenever you need them. We don't impose usage restrictions or require subscriptions.

How do you keep the tools free? ▼

We support the site through non-intrusive ads and optional donations. Your data isn't sold to advertisers - we respect your privacy.

Can I suggest a new tool? ▼

Absolutely! We welcome tool suggestions from our users. Visit our contact page to submit your idea.

Do the tools work on mobile devices? ▼

Yes! All our tools are designed to work perfectly on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
