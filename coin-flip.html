About the Coin Flipper 🪙

The Coin Flipper is a simple tool that helps you make quick decisions. Whether you're choosing between two options, deciding who goes first, or just having fun, this flipper ensures randomness and fairness.

🚀 How to Use This Tool:

  • 🔹 Click the "Flip" button to toss the coin.
  • 🔹 Wait for the result to display either "Heads" or "Tails".
  • 🔹 Use the result to make your decision.
  • 🔹 You can flip again for another random outcome.
